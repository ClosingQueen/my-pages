Property Overview

Cash-Flowing Duplex Near Ball State University

A professionally managed duplex with current income in place, attached garage, front parking, and clear rent upside.

Deal Summary

This 2,181 sq ft duplex has an attached garage and plenty of parking in front. The property is walking distance to the beautiful Minnetrista Museum and Gardens and less than 2 miles from Ball State University.

One unit is a 3 bed / 1 bath currently cash-flowing at $800/month with a month-to-month tenant. The other unit is a 2 bed / 1.5 bath cash-flowing at $775/month, with the tenant recently renewed through the end of February 2027.

Although total monthly income is currently $1,575, there is potential to increase ROI even more. Per Rentometer, average monthly income in this area for a 3/1 should be $1,236 and for a 2/1.5 should be $1,136, creating an average total monthly potential of $2,372.

Taxes for Spring 24/25 and Fall 24/25 were each $778. This property is professionally managed.
